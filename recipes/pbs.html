Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

This peanut butter sandwich recipe is a classic and simple dish that is beloved by many. It involves spreading a generous amount of peanut butter on a slice of bread, and you can add a layer of jelly or jam if desired. It's a quick and satisfying meal or snack that can be enjoyed at any time of the day.

Ingredients

Ordered List of Ingredients

  1. 2 slices of bread (any type)
  2. 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  3. Optional: 1 tablespoon jelly or jam

Unordered List of Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type)
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon jelly or jam
