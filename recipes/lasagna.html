Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of flat lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, a hearty meat sauce, and Parmesan cheese. It's baked to perfection, offering a comforting and satisfying meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

Ordered List of Ingredients

  1. 9 lasagna noodles
  2. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. 1 pound ground beef
  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 2 cups ricotta cheese
  8. 1 egg
  9. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  10. 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  11. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
