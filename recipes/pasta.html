Pasta

Description

This pasta recipe is a classic Italian dish that can be customized in many ways. It involves cooking pasta to perfection and tossing it with a sauce of your choice, such as marinara, alfredo, or pesto. You can add proteins like chicken or shrimp, and vegetables like spinach or mushrooms, to make it a hearty meal.

Ingredients

Ordered List of Ingredients

  1. 1 pound pasta (any type)
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  3. 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon salt
  6. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 2 cups sauce of choice (marinara, alfredo, pesto)
  8. 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  9. Optional: protein (chicken, shrimp) and vegetables (spinach, mushrooms)
